## Inter-Area Routing Loop Prevention Mechanism

OSPF requires that all non-backbone areas be directly connected to area 0 and inter-area routes be advertised through area 0.
Inter-area routes cannot be advertised between two non-backbone areas. As such, a star topology is logically formed in the OSPF area architecture.
